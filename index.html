Please read

Before we arrive, please make sure of a few things so we can give your car the best clean:

  • Water supply — while we detail, we'll need access to the house owner's water supply (an outside tap).
  • Room to work — there must be enough room for us to detail properly on your driveway. If there isn't, we'll need to arrange a better spot with a water supply available to detail.
  • Parking — there must be a suitable place for us to park somewhere near the house we're detailing at, which can include just up the curb.

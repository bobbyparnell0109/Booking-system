# BB Car Detailing — Poster / Leaflet prompt (for "Claude design")

The user is creating a poster/leaflet in an AI design tool. They will supply
**three real images** to drop in: their **logo**, a **photo of Bobby & Bramley**,
and the **QR code** (`bb-qr.png`, in this repo — links to the booking form).

## The prompt (paste into the design tool)

```
Create a premium, print-ready A4 portrait poster for a mobile car detailing
business called "BB Car Detailing". 300 DPI with print bleed. Also output a
square 1080x1080 (Instagram/Facebook) and a portrait 1080x1920 (Stories)
version of the same design.

IMPORTANT — I AM PROVIDING 3 IMAGES. USE THESE EXACT IMAGES IN THE DESIGN. DO
NOT generate, draw, or invent your own versions of them:
  • IMAGE 1 = THE LOGO (silver metallic "BB CAR DETAILING" wordmark with a car
    silhouette). PLACE IT: centered at the very TOP of the poster as the header.
  • IMAGE 2 = PHOTO OF THE TWO OWNERS (two men). PLACE IT: in the MIDDLE of the
    poster, in a clean rounded frame with a subtle silver border, with the
    caption "Meet Bobby & Bramley — your local detailers" underneath.
  • IMAGE 3 = A QR CODE (black on silver). PLACE IT: in the BOTTOM-RIGHT,
    inside a clean light/silver rounded tile so it stands out on the dark
    background. Keep it large enough to scan easily (roughly 3cm on print).
    Do NOT create your own QR code — use the one I provide exactly as-is.

If a placeholder is needed before I upload, leave a clearly labelled empty box
for each ("LOGO HERE", "OWNERS' PHOTO HERE", "QR CODE HERE") in those exact
positions so I can drop the images straight in.

BRAND & MOOD:
- Sleek, high-end, modern automotive feel; dark and luxurious.
- Palette: deep black background (#0c0c0d) with brushed-metal silver/chrome
  accents and crisp white text; subtle metallic gradients and soft light
  reflections like a freshly polished car under showroom lights.
- Clean, uncluttered, lots of negative space, readable from across a room.
- Bold modern condensed sans-serif for headlines.

TEXT & LAYOUT (top to bottom):
1. TOP: IMAGE 1 (the logo).
2. Tagline headline, bold: "CLEANER. SHINIER. BETTER."
3. Sub-line: "Professional mobile car detailing — we come to you."
4. MIDDLE: IMAGE 2 (owners' photo) with its caption.
5. SERVICES & PRICES panel (clear and legible):
     • Exterior Wash — £25  (snow foam, hand wash, wheels, tyres dressed,
       streak-free finish)
     • Interior Clean — £25  (full vacuum, dashboard, door cards, glass and
       surfaces wiped down)
     • Full Valet — £40  (inside and out — our most popular)
6. Small badges/icons: "We come to you" · "Evening & weekend appointments" ·
   "Book online in seconds".
7. BOTTOM CALL TO ACTION:
     - Left side: bold "BOOK ONLINE" plus contact details:
         Bobby: 07903 512940
         Bramley: 07434 651512
         Find & message us on Facebook
         bobbyparnell0109.github.io/Booking-system
     - Right side: IMAGE 3 (the QR code) in its silver tile.

Double-check all text is spelled correctly and the prices (£25, £25, £40) and
phone numbers appear exactly as written above.
```
